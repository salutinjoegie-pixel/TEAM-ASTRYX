Marshmallow Challenge

Overview

The Marshmallow Challenge is a fun team-building activity where participants build the tallest free-standing structure using spaghetti, tape, and string, with a marshmallow placed on top.

Objective

Encourage teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving under time pressure.

How It Works

  • Teams are given materials (spaghetti, tape, string, marshmallow).
  • They have limited time (usually 18 minutes).
  • The tallest structure with the marshmallow on top wins.

Reflection

During the Marshmallow Challenge, our group first focused on building the tallest structure using spaghetti, tape, string, and a marshmallow. We were excited, but the foundation was weak, so the spaghetti broke and our structure collapsed. Determined to try again, we built it shorter this time, thinking it would be more stable but it still collapsed! Hahaha😅. Even though we failed twice, we learned a lot from the experience. We realized that planning carefully, testing ideas, and making a strong foundation are just as important as aiming high. The challenge also taught us about teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving. Most importantly, it reminded us that failure is part of learning, and it’s okay to laugh, try again, and improve as a team.
