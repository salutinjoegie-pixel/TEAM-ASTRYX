Persona Mapping

REFLECTION

Creating the persona mapping for our Solar-Powered Piso WiFi system helped us better understand the needs, goals, and challenges of our target users. At first, it was a bit difficult to think of realistic situations and user experiences, but as we continued, we were able to identify common problems such as difficulty in monitoring the coin box and limited access to electricity. Through this activity, we realized that different users have different priorities, but they all need a system that is reliable, affordable, and easy to manage.
