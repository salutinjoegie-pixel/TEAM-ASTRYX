Jeoparody quiz

Overview

The Jeopardy Quiz Activity is a game-based learning activity inspired by the Jeopardy TV show. It makes lesson review more engaging by allowing participants to choose questions by category and points. The activity promotes teamwork, quick thinking, and active participation while making learning fun and less stressful.


Objective

  • To make learning enjoyable and reduce academic pressure
  • To review and reinforce previously discussed lessons in an interactive way
  • To encourage active participation and engagement among students
  • To develop critical thinking and quick decision-making skills


Reflection

Our Jeopardy activity was still a fun and enjoyable experience even though we were not able to answer the questions correctly. Despite this, we remained active and motivated throughout the game, especially because of the excitement brought by the competition and the GCash prize. Even though we got zero points, we still appreciated the opportunity to participate and learn.
