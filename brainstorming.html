BRAINSTORMING

Overview

Brainstorming is a group activity where participants generate ideas to solve a problem or create something new.

Objective

  • Encourage creativity, collaboration
  • open thinking.

Reflection

During the brainstorming activity, our group initially struggled because we didn’t have any clear ideas. It was difficult to start, and there were moments of silence since no one was sure what to suggest. We felt a bit confused and unsure of what direction to take. However, as time went on, we slowly began sharing small ideas, even if we weren’t confident about them. This helped us break the silence and encouraged others to speak up.
